How can using an EHR benefit my practice?

   1. Avoid workflow disruptions caused by manual patient charts
   2. Cut down valuable time spent digging through hard-to-parse records
   3. Ensure stronger security surrounding patient info
   4. Increase mobility with EHR-Enabled Mobile devices

How does an EHR system improve patient care?

   1.Physicians spending less time manually charting patients writing prescriptions and accessing patient history, more time is invested where it matters – in caring for patients.
   2.Reference previous notes
   3. Participate in increased interoperability
   4. Boost patient to physician communication and vice versa
